# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 22, 2026**

BudgetBoss ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service (collectively, the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account and use BudgetBoss, we collect:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and password (encrypted)
- **Financial Data**: Bank account names, transaction descriptions, amounts, dates, and categories that you enter or import
- **Budget Information**: Budget names, amounts, and categories you create
- **Savings Goals**: Goal names, target amounts, and progress data

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App feature usage, session duration, and interaction patterns
- **Push Notification Tokens**: If you enable notifications, we store your device's push token

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT have access to your actual bank credentials
- We do NOT connect to your bank accounts automatically
- We do NOT sell or share your financial data with third parties
- We do NOT use tracking or advertising SDKs

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Process and display your financial transactions and budgets
- Send push notifications (with your consent) for reminders and milestones
- Calculate gamification features (XP, levels, streaks, badges)
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized access

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Self-Hosted Architecture

BudgetBoss is designed as a **self-hosted application**. This means:

- Your data is stored in **your own PostgreSQL database**
- You control where your database is hosted
- No financial data is stored on our servers unless you use our hosted version

### 3.2 Security Measures

- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text
- Mobile authentication uses JWT tokens stored in device secure storage
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- CSV imports are processed in memory and not written to disk

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** share your personal or financial information with third parties, except:

- When required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or the safety of users
- With your explicit consent

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Portability

You can view all your data within the app at any time.

### 5.2 Correction

You can edit or update your account information and financial data through the app.

### 5.3 Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings screen in the mobile app. This action is irreversible and will delete:

- Your user account
- All transactions
- All bank accounts
- All budgets
- All savings goals
- All badges and gamification data
- All push notification tokens

### 5.4 Push Notifications

You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy

BudgetBoss is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 8. Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
- Deleted accounts: All data is permanently deleted immediately upon account deletion
- We do not retain backups of deleted user data

## 9. International Users

If you access the Service from outside the country where your self-hosted database is located, please be aware that your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in that location.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

**Email**: privacy@budgetboss.app
